Parametric triggers

Four events.
Automatic protection.

Each trigger is tied to real, verifiable environmental data — not a claim assessor's opinion.

🌧
Heavy Rainfall
Rainfall > 40 mm
Monsoon rains reduce delivery demand and make riding hazardous. ZeptoShield activates the moment your city's rainfall exceeds the safety threshold.
🌡
Extreme Heat
Temperature > 42°C
Heatwaves pose serious health risks for anyone working outdoors. When the mercury climbs past the threshold, your protection kicks in automatically.
🏭
Severe Air Quality
AQI > 350
At AQI levels above 350, outdoor work becomes a serious health risk. Our system monitors real-time air quality data across all cities, continuously.
🌊
Flood Warning
Government alert issued
When official flood alerts are issued, roads become inaccessible and deliveries impossible. We monitor government data feeds and trigger payouts the moment alerts go out.
