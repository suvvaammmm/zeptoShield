The process

From signup to
payout — in seconds.

ZeptoShield removes all friction from income protection. No manual claims, no waiting, no back-and-forth with assessors.

01
Register your profile
Enter your name, phone number, and city. Your account is created instantly. No documents needed, no identity verification delays.
02
AI risk assessment
Our system analyses your city's historical weather patterns, flood records, and pollution data to assign a risk profile — Low, Medium, or High.
03
Review your premium
Your weekly premium is shown upfront — ₹20, ₹30, or ₹40 — based purely on your city's risk level. No hidden fees, no variable pricing.
04
Activate with one tap
Confirm your policy in a single click. Coverage starts immediately and begins monitoring your city's environmental data around the clock.
05
Continuous monitoring
ZeptoShield tracks weather APIs, real-time AQI feeds, and government flood alert systems for your city — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
06
Instant automatic payout
When a trigger threshold is crossed, your claim is auto-approved and ₹1,500 is credited to your Zepto wallet. No action required on your part.
